Data Privacy and Security Policy

Document ID: POL-DATA-002
Effective Date: March 15, 2025

1. Objective
To ensure customer and company data is handled safely and legally.

2. Data Classification
All company data is classified into two categories:

• Public: Information freely available to anyone (e.g., marketing materials).
• Confidential: Customer data, employee records, and financial data.

3. Data Protection Rules

• Confidential data must not be sent to personal email addresses.
• Databases containing customer information should be encrypted.
• Employees working remotely must use the company VPN to access confidential files.
